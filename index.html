About us

Probably the best place to enjoy your life

When you're searching for a low price on luxury hotels, The Siron Place is well identified with a wide range of options, with offers ranging from small meeting rooms to a large banquet sized function rooms with complimentary facilities such as mineral water and toiletries., Our stylish hotel also offers free high speed Wi-Fi which is available in all rooms alongside High-end CCTV cameras of which are also installed accompanied with security guards to ensure safety of clients. In addition, large spacious rooms each with Queen-sized beds, hot shower and room services are reliable and conveniently placed.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner services are also offered at specific time frames that will be displayed. We believe you won't find a better publicly available rate for our hotels anywhere else.
